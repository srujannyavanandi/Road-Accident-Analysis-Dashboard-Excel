# Road Accident Analysis Dashboard

### Project Overview

The Road Accident Analysis Dashboard is an interactive business intelligence dashboard designed to analyze road accident data and provide actionable insights into accident severity, casualty distribution, road conditions, vehicle involvement, and environmental factors.

The dashboard enables stakeholders, transportation authorities, and policymakers to identify accident patterns, evaluate risk factors, and support data-driven road safety initiatives.

### Objectives
- Monitor overall road accident casualties.
- Analyze accident severity levels.
- Identify vehicle types contributing to casualties.
- Compare yearly casualty trends.
- Evaluate the impact of road types and road surface conditions.
- Analyze casualties based on area type and lighting conditions.
- Enable dynamic filtering for deeper investigation

### Key Business Insights
- Slight severity accidents account for over 83% of total casualties. 
- Cars are involved in the majority of accident casualties. 
- Single carriageways are the most accident-prone road type. 
- Urban areas experience significantly more casualties than rural areas. 
- Most accidents occur during daylight conditions. 
- Dry road surfaces show the highest casualty count due to greater traffic exposure. 
- Monthly casualty trends remain relatively consistent across years.
